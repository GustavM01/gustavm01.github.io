My name is Jim Carrey. I am an actor and standup comedian from Newmarket, Ontario, Canada, but I became an U.S sitizen in 2004.

I am known for my acting in movies, such as the Ace Ventura movie series, Dumb and Dumber, The Cable Guy, Liar Liar and Bruce Almighty. I have also been successful in dramatic roles in movies such as The Truman Show, Man on the Moon, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind and The Number 23.
